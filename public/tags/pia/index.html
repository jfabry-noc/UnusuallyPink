Chromebooks, VPNs, and You

2019-09-13 :: John Fabry
#chrome  #chrome-os  #google  #linux  #pia  #private-internet-access  #tunnelbear  #vpn 
Do you trust your Internet service provider? If you answer that question with a resounding “No!”, then you’re among friends here. While Internet connectivity is an essential need for many of us, there are a plethora of reasons to be wary of the company providing that connectivity to you. Especially in the United States, the recent repeal of net neutrality rules and erosion of privacy mean you may want to obscure exactly what you’re doing from your Internet provider, even if that activity is completely legal and innocuous.
