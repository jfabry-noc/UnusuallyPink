Trying Out NextDNS

2020-10-31 :: John Fabry
#adblocking  #dns  #nextdns  #privacy 
I recently decided to take a little bit of time to set up NextDNS. While on the surface it’s similar to the myriad other DNS resolvers out there you can opt to use instead of the likely horrible DNS servers provided by your ISP, NextDNS is essentially a cloud-based Pi-hole. You can use some of the built-in blacklists in the product to block things like advertising, trackers, malware domains, and more.
